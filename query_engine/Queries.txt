Queries needed

1)   One parameter, one date, multiple stations (Q1)
2)   One station, one parameter, multiple dates (Q2)
3)   Average over time (Q3)
 
Example questions

Historical

1. What is the temperature across all stations for a given time (spatial axis)
2. What is the evolution of temperature over time (temporal axis)
3. If it rains on the 27th of June, does it rain more for the next 4 weeks (proverb testing)

Comparative

4. Which service provider gives the most reliable predictions?
5. For a given provider, which locations are most reliable to predict a weather parameter?
6. Which day of the week provides the best predictions?
